\section{Method}
\label{sec:method}

Let $f \colon \R^n \to \R$ be a twice-differentiable objective.
We seek
%
\begin{equation}
  \label{eq:argmin}
  x^\star = \argmin_{x \in \R^n}\, f(x).
\end{equation}
%
The gradient is $\nabla f(x) \in \R^n$ and the Hessian is $\nabla^2 f(x) \in \R^{n \times n}$.
When $f$ is $\mu$-strongly convex, the condition number $\kappa = L / \mu$ governs convergence.
Standard bracket notation from \texttt{physics2}: $\ab|x|$ and $\ab(x + y)$.
